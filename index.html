Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favorite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bootmark in one click.

Organize your bootmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favorite sites.

Intelligent search

Our powerful search feature will help you find saved sited in not time at all. No need to trawl through all of your bookmarks.

Share your bootmarks

Easily share your bootmarks and collections with others. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button
